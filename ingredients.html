What's "real" Mexican food, anyway?

Cuisines are constantly evolving, never set in stone for eternity. So words like "real" or "authentic" are always loaded. But we'll do our best to get to the heart of authentic Mexican cuisine and lay out a diverse spread of recipes for you to savor and explore.

Mix it all up, add time, and you get Mexican cuisine as we know (and love) it today.
